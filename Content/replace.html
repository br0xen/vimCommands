Replace Commands

vim does searches by regular expressions

:%s/old/new/g
Replace all occurences of old with new
:3,6s/old/new/g
Replace all occurences of old with new between the lines of 3 and 6
:3,$s/old/new/g
Replace all occurences of old with new between the lines of 3 and the end of the file
:g/word/d
Delete all lines containing word
:v/word/d
Delete all lines NOT containing word
:%s#<[^>]\+>##g
Remove HTML tags but keep text in between them
Ctrl+a
Increment the number under the cursor
Ctrl+x
Decrement the number under the cursor
ggVGg?
Rot13 the entire buffer
